    Target's Payment Policy

    Immediate payment is required upon selecting "Buy It Now".

    Methods

    We only accept immediate payment through the eBay checkout system. We only accept payment via US PayPal accounts. PayPal allows you to pay using a major credit card or direct transfer from your checking account.

    Sales Tax

    Sales Tax Information

    Target charges sales tax in all U.S. states except Delaware, Montana, New Hampshire, and Oregon (these states don't have a sales tax).  The amount of tax charged for your order depends upon certain factors, including:

    • The selling price of the item purchased
    • The type of item purchased
    • The destination of the shipment

    To identify whether your order has been taxed, visit your eBay Order Details page.

    How Sales Tax is Calculated

    If an item is subject to sales or use tax in the state to which it is shipped, tax is generally calculated on its total selling price.  In accordance with state laws, the total selling price of an item generally includes item-level shipping and handling charges, discounts and gift-wrap charges, and an allocation of order-level shipping and handling charges and discounts.

    The tax rate applied to your order will generally be the combined state and local rate for the address where your order is approved or shipped.  Therefore, the tax rate applied to your order may be different for an order shipped to your home address than it is for an order for the very same items shipped to your work address.

    Estimated Tax

    Many factors can change between the time you place an order and the time your order is shipped.  Therefore, the amount appearing on your order as "Estimated Tax" may differ from the sales taxes ultimately charged. 

    Promotions and Discounts

    Target promotional codes, including Redcard discounts, are not valid on the eBay site.  However, many current Target promotions will appear in our eBay store - no promotional code necessary!

    Shipping Policy

    Domestic Shipping:

    We ship within the 48 contiguous US states, plus Washington DC. Please note that we are not able to ship to Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico, US Territories, PO Boxes or APO/FPO addresses.

    We currently offer standard, expedited, or economy (oversized) shipping only. Please allow up to 2 business days for processing and handling.

    If you selected economy shipping for oversized items, the service for your delivery will be To the Door. The item will be placed outside your home in the first available dry area. You do not need to be present for delivery. Unpacking and debris removal are not included

    Please keep in mind, once an order has been submitted, our fulfillment center begins processing it right away so we are not able to modify shipping addresses, items, or guarantee a cancellation of your order.

    International Shipping:

    Target Store on eBay ships internationally to many countries. The expedited shipping option applies only for domestic shipping between Target and eBay facilities within the US. Once your order leaves eBay's US facility to be shipped internationally, eBay international shipping methods and any associated shipping charges will apply.

    Please note that free shipping promotions apply only to the above domestic shipping portion of internationally shipped orders. We currently do not offer free shipping for international shipping charges.

    Please keep in mind, once an order has been submitted, our fulfillment center begins processing it right away so we are not able to modify shipping addresses, items, or guarantee a cancellation of your order.

    Refund/Exchange Policy:

    Domestic Policy:

    Most unopened items in new condition and returned within 30 days from the ship date will receive a refund or exchange. Items purchased between 11/1-12/25, may be returned up to 60 days from the ship date.

    Items that are opened or damaged or do not have a packing slip or receipt may be denied a refund or exchange.

    Most purchases can be returned to a U.S. Target store, just bring in your item and the packing slip that came with it to the Guest Service Desk for a refund or exchange. All Paypal purchased items that are returned to a Target store will be refunded to cash.

    If you choose to mail in your items, please call us at 612-307-8330 or click Contact Us on the Target storefront page, and we will help you return your item to our Return Center. You will receive a refund to your Paypal account once the product has been received and processed (usually within 7-10 days from the day we receive your return). Target does not currently offer exchanges through the mail return process, but you may exchange for a similar item at any U.S. Target store.

    Target stores cannot accept international returns or exchanges, including international returns and exchanges of online purchases.

    International Policy:

    Items returned within 30 days from the ship date or 28 days of delivery (whichever is the latest) will receive a refund. Items purchased between 1 November and 15 January, may be returned up to 60 days from the ship date.

    To return your items, please call us at 1- 612-307-8330 or click Contact Us on the Target storefront page, and we will help you return your item to our Return Center (please allow an additional day for response due to time zone differences). You will receive a refund to your PayPal account no later than 14 days from the day we receive your return at our returns facility or you provide evidence of returned goods. Target does not currently offer exchanges through the mail return process.

    Buyer is responsible for return shipping expense and is required to fill out all necessary customs paperwork prior to shipping.

    U.S Target stores cannot accept international returns or exchanges of online purchases.
